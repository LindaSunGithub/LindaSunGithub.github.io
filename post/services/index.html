Foreign Property and Income Verification


The Canadian government identifies the following as overseas assets, which are funds held outside Canada. Shares of non-resident corporations (other than foreign affiliates), indebtedness owed by non-residents, interests in non-resident trusts, real property outside Canada, other property outside Canada, and property held in an account with a Canadian registered securities dealer or a Canadian trust company.
