About us

Linda Sun acquired her Bachelor Degree of Accounting in 1991. She then started her professional accounting career, working as a senior accountant and controller in various private and public corporations throughout her initial years. Her own accounting firm, Linda Sun Consulting, was established in 2008. She now has over 3 decades of professional work experience, giving her exceptional strategies which she applies daily to her public practice, and offers professional and timely comprehensive services. She enjoys working closely with her clients, assisting them in all the financial aspects of their business affairs.



Our practice provides taxation, accounting, and business consulting services. We offer a wide range of services to small and medium size companies in a variety of industries, businesses, and professions. And we place a special focus on real estate development, investment holding, import and export trade, e-commerce, manufacturing and distribution of wholesale, travel agencies, hotels and restaurants, supermarkets and retail and other fields.



Linda Sun is a member of the Chartered Professional Accountant Association of Canada (CPA) and the Chartered Professional Accountant Association of British Columbia (CPA BC).
